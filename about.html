Zaney Tarot

Zaney Tarot is a free anime-inspired tarot reading web app built around visual card styles, shuffleable spreads, card meanings, and whole-reading interpretations.

The readings are designed for reflection, creativity, and playful self-check-ins. They are not professional medical, legal, financial, or mental health advice.

The project also includes print-ready Anime Arcana deck assets for exploring physical card production alongside the web reading experience.
